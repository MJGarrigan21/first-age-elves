Lúthien Tinúviel

Princess of Doriath

Of all the Half-Elven, Luthien was the most powerful in so many ways. She was the only Child of Illuvatar to born child of a Maiar.

Her parents were Elu Thingol, King of the Sindar and Doriath, Lord of all Beleriand, and Melian the Maia of Illuvatar.

Lúthien's story is one of the most beautiful and important in all the history of Arda.

Some Greatest Feats
  • Can awaken Spring from winter through her beauty and song.
  • Threw down the walls of Minas Tirith after saving Beren from the dungeons of Sauron.
  • Sang a song of rest so powerful the court of Morgoth, including the Dark Lord himself, were put into a deep and shadowy slumber.
