Glorfindel

Chief of the House of the Golden Flower

Glorfindel was a follower of Turgon, and a great Lord of the Noldor- mighty amongst the Gondolindrim.

He saved many of the last survivors of the Fall of Gondolin, sacrificing himself in the process.

Due to his purity of heart and intentions, he was sent back to Middle Earth by the Valar in the Second Age

Some Greatest Feats
  • Killing a Balrog and dying in the process.
  • Saving the life of young Earendil, future hope and savior of the people's of Beleriand.
  • Returned to Middle Earth and empowered as a beacon of good by the Valar.
