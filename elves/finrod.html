Finrod Felagund

King of Nargothrond and a Lord of the Noldor.

Felagund earned his name from the Dwarves, meaning "Maker of Caves", for his crafting of the city Nargothrond from the caves of the river Narog.

Finrod was also the first of the Caliquendi, the High Elves, to find the Edain- the first of mankind to cross into Beleriand.

Some Greatest Feats
  • Crossing the Helcaraxe
  • Carving Nargothrond from the river Narog
  • Battled Sauron through songs of power.
